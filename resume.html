Yinqiong Cai

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. Here, I’m luckily enough to be advised by Prof. Jiafeng Guo and Yixing Fan. I am a member of CAS Key Lab of Web Data Science and Technology. My research interests lie in Information Retrieval, with a special focus on dense retrieval.

Prior to this, I earned my Bachelor’s degree at School of Software Technology, Dalian University of Technology, advised by Assoc Prof. Xiangjie Kong. My research interests include big data, mobile computing, and computational social science.
